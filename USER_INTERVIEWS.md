Interview Strategy & Planning (Days 1-3)The goal for these interviews is to validate the core problem: Startups pay for AI tools but have no internal benchmark or "second opinion" on their spend.   Target PersonasThe Solo Founder / CTO: Managing all seats manually; likely over-subscribed to "Pro" tiers for individual needs.   The Engineering Manager: Managing a team of 10+; likely has a mix of Cursor, GitHub Copilot, and Claude Team seats with high overlap.   The Operations Lead: Responsible for the "SaaS stack"; looks at the monthly bill but doesn't understand tool-specific technical redundancies.   Standard Interview Script"How many different AI subscriptions (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) does your team currently pay for?"   "When was the last time you checked if every 'Team' seat is actually being used by a team member?"   "If I told you that switching one tool could save you $200/month, what would be your first concern or barrier to switching?"   "What is your current process for deciding between an API-direct plan versus a per-seat subscription?"
