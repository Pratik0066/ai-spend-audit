### North Star Metric
**Audit-to-Lead Conversion Rate:** The percentage of users who land on the tool and successfully submit their email to unlock the AI analysis[cite: 39, 41].

### Input Metrics
1. **Average Savings Identified:** A higher average savings amount correlates directly with lead quality for Credex[cite: 30].
2. **Viral Share Multiplier:** How many new audits are generated per unique shareable URL shared[cite: 93].
3. **Time-to-Value (TTV):** The number of seconds from landing to seeing the "Hero" savings cards.

### Pivot Trigger
If the **Consultation Booking Rate** for users with >$500/mo savings is less than 2%, I would pivot the landing copy to focus less on "audits" and more on "direct credit access".
